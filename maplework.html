For the independent professional, MapleWork offers freedom of mind and space to build your brand - individual or consumer facing - housed on a tree-lined campus nestled inside the safe, conveniently located suburb of Maplewood. Maplewood is a vibrant, creative metropolis, offering collaboration within an existing consultative culture focused on growing national brands with professional research tools and strategic minds.

Who We Are

MapleWork takes its name from its community - Maplewood. The suburban town possesses a vibrant main street situated on the fringe of suburbia and located between St. Louis’ two main highway arteries that connect suburbs with the city. Maplewood claims it’s “somewhere between Mayberry and Metropolis.” Free, accessible parking complements the MapleWork location situated amidst a thriving restaurant and bar scene. In addition to location, the MapleWork space has everything millennials expect from a workplace - wifi, coffee, beer on tap, access to a kitchen, printers, and a pet friendly policy. The space occupies the first floor of a renovated school attached to a Church. Residing in the Church and floors above is a marketing agency, Moosylvania, whose CEO Norty Cohen owns and operates both buildings. Norty values innovation and understands how collaboration contributes to the art of branding - consumer facing or professional.
