Who We Are

Nestled in a tree-lined campus, MapleWork brings all the excitement of the city, with the safety and convenience of the Maplewood area. Join the network of national brands and strategic thinkers, while enjoying access to the neighborhood’s unique restaurants, one of a kind shops, bustling breweries and craft coffee houses. In addition to location, the MapleWork space provides comforts such as wifi, coffee, free parking, printers and additional meeting rooms.

02

Where We Are

The lively community of Maplewood offers a vibrant main street that is the hub of award-winning food purveyors and craft brewers. Voted as one of the top “hipster” markets in America, Maplewood takes pride in its local businesses and neighborhood events. Situated between St. Louis’ two main highways that connect the suburbs with the city, the town claims it’s “somewhere between Mayberry and Metropolis.”

From the MapleWork office, enjoy a short stroll to:

  • Schlafly Bottleworks
  • US Postal Service
  • Foundation Grounds
  • The Side Project Cellar

  • Much, much more!
